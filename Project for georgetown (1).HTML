Why We Chose Our Topic?

We chose the topic of video games because all 5 of us play them and it was one of the more interesting topics to us out of the options we could have chosen. The question we proposed and decided to research was “What makes a video game successful?”. The game that motivated us to pick that question was the rapid, and recent success of the game Fortnite. I’m sure you all have heard of that.
Other reasons for picking the topic is because the industry is constantly burgeoning. In the recent past the industry is starting to reach heights that were never thought to be tangible. The market is constantly growing with new players buying games everyday and joining the community. The industry provides you an opportunity to show your creative and analytical skills. The game designers are responsible for conceptualizing the theme, rules of the video game, and the structure of the game. Being analytical and organized are skills that you absolutely need.

    • The Plots:

  • Between total players and price there is a negative correlation, which means as the price of a game increases in general the amount of player lessens. This is an obvious relationship since people won’t want to purchase an outrageously expensive game. Finding the right price balance to appease the player base and make a profit is a struggle video game companies have to battle to be successful.

  • The relationship between rating and total players has a strong positive correlation. This can be seen since almost no games rated on Steam between 0-80 have a large player base compared to games that are rated,which see an 80+ see a large increase in player base. On Steam this shows that potential success heavily relies on community opinion, which means companies should prioritize making their rating high.

    • Our Answer:


    As a result of the data we found and analyzed we concluded that there is not one quantitative element that makes a video game successful, it is a combination of multiple elements. Some of the components of a successful video game are no to little cost, the low price of the game is offset by the player bases willingness to buy in-app purchases. The ability to play with friends and having a good rating by current players to persuade more potential players to play also play an important role in measuring the success of a videogame. Although these are some of the most crucial aspects for a video game to include, there are still some qualitative factors of a potential video game that can make it successful, but these types of factors cannot be put into a proper data set. So things like how interesting a games main story line is or the individual difficulty of each game cannot be quantified properly in data.
